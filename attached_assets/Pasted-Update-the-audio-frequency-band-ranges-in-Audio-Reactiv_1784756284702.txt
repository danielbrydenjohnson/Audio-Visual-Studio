GOAL

Change the band ranges to:

20 Hz to 150 Hz

150 Hz to 350 Hz

High:

The 150 Hz to 350 Hz range should not feed Low, Mid, or High.

This gap is intentional.

Low should focus on:

- kick
- sub
- bass weight
- low-end pressure

Mid should focus on:

- snare
- clap
- vocal body
- synth body
- musical articulation

The 150 Hz to 350 Hz low-mid area can make visuals feel muddy, overactive, or confused. Excluding it should make the band separation cleaner.
